In 2018 we created the visual identity of the spanish advertising awards festival CdeC, held in San Sebastián.

"Bonita Simbiosis" would like to show a place where EVERYBODY is important and all the job profiles are needed to carry out an advertising campaign. A print where all the sector is shown, from the directors to the camera auxiliars, account executives or the trainees. Even the briefings and coffee.
