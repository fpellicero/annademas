Bona Nit Barcelona: L'arbre dels desitjos
WallPeople

I collabored as an illustrator with WallPeople in Bona Nit Barcelona, an ecological event which focuses on sustainability, healthy food and fair trade. 

"L'arbre dels desitjos" (The Tree of wishes) is an idea of Elisabet Suñé that consists in inviting people to share their advices, desires and ideas about how to make Barcelona become a greener city. A lot of people came and wrote down in paper leaves their wishes. They were all hung on the tree branches of the wall.
