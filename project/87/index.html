Scriptorivm

Scriptorivm was a medieval room where the scribes did manuscripts. Now, my Scriptorivm is a tattoo cabin and my manuscripts are inked pieces of art on skin. My work is inspired in different styles: Blackwork, linework, dotwork, etching and engraving (in a medieval way).

In 2016/2017 I studied a tattoo formation in "ARA Educació School". Nowadays I rent a tattoo cabin in some studios in my free time to keep practicing and tattoo people.

If you want to take a look at my work on skin, please visit my Instagram @annademas.
