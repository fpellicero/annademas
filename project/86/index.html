Club Born Combat Medieval

Born Combat Medieval is a sportive club based in Catalunya. It is one of the spanish teams of historical medieval full contact fighting that participates in tournaments around the world.

I have collaborated in the design of its graphical identity, including the creation of promotional posters, tri folds, instagram posts, logo replica bell ross animation and even the realization and edition of some videos of their tournaments.
