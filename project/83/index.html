¡No más cafés en el aire! (ALDI)
The Backroom

We are sure that you have a friend who always proposes to have a coffee and you never do it. Now it’s your time to end with all those coffees in the air...in the air. In a hot-air balloon, exactly.
I designed the digital comunication for the Facebook contest (Win a weekend experience in Segovia with a friend having coffee in a hot-air balloon) and edited the videos below about the invited bloggers experience.  You can read their articles here: Dansvogue, Las Maria Cocinillas, La Cocina de Carolina, Madresfera).
