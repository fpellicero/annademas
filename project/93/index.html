Malquerida - Estrella Damm

Creative Director: Carles Alcon
Copy: Guillem Gutiérrez
Art Direction: Anna de Mas, David Bartolomé

Malquerida is a new style of beer made by Ferran & Albert Adrià and the Beer Masters of Damm, perfect for pairing with latin food (extreme, powerful and spicy dishes). 

In Sant Jordi, the Love and Book Day, we offered spicy stories when people ordered a Malquerida in "Sant Jordi Musical Fest" in l'Antiga Fàbrica Damm. Spicy Stories. Literally. Five spicy "totopos" with a written story in their packs, each one more spicy than the other, in flavour and in story. The erotic stories were written by Alba Ribas and the Totopos were made by Oaxaca BCN latin restaurant. Happy and Exciting Sant Jordi.
