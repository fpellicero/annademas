"Humor a Cara i Creu" is a documentary produced by Lemure about the evolution of Catalan Graphic Humor. I participated as the creator and illustrator of the character who tells the story, an old wise man with young spirit and funny manners. The character will never have an aesthetic of finished drawing, but will be inspired in the typical draft strokes. It was animated by Alejandro.
