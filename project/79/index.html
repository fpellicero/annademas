Tattooed Notebooks
Personal project

'Tattooed Notebooks' began when I started to draw in my McCann workmates' notebooks. Each drawing in their covers represents one aspect of their personalities and life circumstances. Therefore, I tattooed their notebooks as people do with symbols in their skins.
